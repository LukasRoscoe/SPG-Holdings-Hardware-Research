Connecticut, Massachusetts & Rhode Island - Ranked for Sump Pump Campaign

Analysis Methodology & Ranking System

This comprehensive analysis includes hardware stores across Connecticut, Massachusetts, and Rhode Island, ranked by their potential for successful sump pump campaigns. Each store has been evaluated using verified data from official sources:

Median Income: From U.S. Census Bureau American Community Survey (2021-2022)
Annual Rainfall: From NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information (10-year averages)
Flood Risk: From First Street Foundation & FEMA Flood Map Service Center
Competition Analysis: Based on Google Maps driving distance and time to nearest Home Depot and Lowe's

Stores are scored 0-100 based on a weighted formula: Flood Risk (25%), Rainfall (20%), Income Levels (25%), and Distance from Competitors (30%).
